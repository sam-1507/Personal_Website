Project 1 : Online Voting System

Problem Statement :
Traditional voting systems face challenges such as inefficiency, security risks, and logistical issues. Voters often experience long queues, errors, and a lack of transparency, leading to disenfranchisement and fraud. There is a growing need for a secure, accessible, and user-friendly online voting platform that ensures transparency, privacy, and integrity in the voting process.

How it works :
Registration: Users sign up with their details (email, password).
Login: Voters log in securely.
Vote: They select their choice (like a candidate) on the webpage.
Submit: The vote is sent and saved securely.
Confirmation: Voters get a message confirming their vote.
Results: After voting ends, results are counted and shown.
